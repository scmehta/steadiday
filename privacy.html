Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 14, 2025

Introduction

SteadiDay supports adults ages 50 to 70 with daily tasks, medications, and connection tools. Privacy matters. This policy explains collected information, usage, and user rights.

What Information Is Collected

Account Information

Name, email address, and login details provided during account creation.

Health Data

Medication schedules, task lists, and health notes entered by the user.

Apple Health Data Optional

With user permission, the app reads selected Apple Health data. Data types include blood type, height, weight, steps, heart rate, sleep hours, exercise minutes, and blood pressure. Access occurs only after Apple Health integration is enabled and permissions are granted. Allergies, medical conditions, emergency contacts, and organ donor status stay inaccessible due to Apple restrictions.

Contact Information

Emergency contacts and favorite contacts added by the user.

Location Data

Location used for weather updates and location-based reminders. Access requires permission.

Device Information

Device type, operating system, and app version used for technical support and stability.

How Information Is Used

  • Medication reminders and task notifications
  • Data sync across user devices
  • Emergency alerts sent to designated contacts
  • App improvement and issue resolution
  • Local weather updates
  • Display of Apple Health wellness metrics when enabled

Data Storage and Security

Data stays stored on the device and within encrypted cloud storage. Security measures include encryption, secure authentication, and routine security reviews. Health information is never sold. Apple Health data flows through the Apple HealthKit framework and stays protected by iOS security and device authentication.

Connected Apps and Data Sharing

Third-party connections such as Apple Health or Google Calendar access only required data. All connections start turned off. User action enables each connection. Disconnection stays available through Settings.

Apple Health Integration

Apple Health integration requests permission through system prompts. Approved data includes blood type, height, weight, steps, heart rate, sleep hours, exercise minutes, and blood pressure. Permission removal stays available through iPhone Settings, Privacy, Health, then SteadiDay. Apple Health data stays local unless cloud sync is enabled. No third-party sharing occurs.

Important Notice

SteadiDay does not function as a medical device. The app does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent disease. Health data serves informational and wellness tracking purposes only. Medical decisions require guidance from a licensed healthcare professional.

Privacy Rights

Access

Users request a copy of stored data.

Correction

Users update personal information within the app.

Deletion

Users request account and data removal.

Portability

Users export data in a standard format.

Opt-Out

Users disable notifications, location access, or analytics at any time.

Cookies and Tracking

Advertising cookies and tracking technologies stay unused. Anonymous usage data supports app improvement. Opt-out remains available in Settings.

Subscription and Payment Information

Premium subscriptions process through the Apple App Store. Payment details remain with Apple. SteadiDay receives subscription status only.

Children and Privacy

The app targets adults ages 50 to 70. Information from individuals under age 18 is not knowingly collected.

Policy Changes

Policy updates occur as needed. Significant updates trigger in-app or email notices. Continued app use signals acceptance of updated terms.

Contact

Questions about privacy or data receive support through the Feedback feature in Settings.
