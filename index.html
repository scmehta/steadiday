Why Families Choose SteadiDay

The worries you've already had

SteadiDay was built around the questions that wake adult children up at night.

"What if she falls and can't reach her phone?"

SteadiDay's SOS is reachable from her home screen. One press: 911 is called, and you get a text with her exact GPS location. Automatic fall detection works in the background — if she falls and doesn't respond, the alert still goes out.

"Did she take her medication today?"

Gentle reminders on her phone. She sees what she's taken. If she chooses to share a Care Summary, you can see she's doing well — no surveillance, no nagging texts back and forth.

"I don't want to set up another account for her."

You won't. No accounts, no passwords, no subscriptions. Install the app on her iPhone, set your number as the emergency contact, and you're done. Five minutes.

Emergency SOS

Medication Reminders

Never miss a dose. Gentle reminders at the right time, with a clear log of what's been taken.

Care Summary

Daily Tasks

Simple, easy-to-read task lists for daily routines, errands, and appointments. Check items off as you go.

Questions?

Frequently asked questions

Is my information private?

Yes. All your data stays on your iPhone and is protected by your phone's security. We never see or store your information on our servers.

Do I need to create an account?

No account needed. Just download and start using it right away. Your data is stored safely on your device.

Is SteadiDay really free?

Yes! Every feature is completely free. We want everyone to experience the full app and share feedback so we can make it even better.

Can my family see my information?

Your data is private. You choose who to add as trusted contacts, and they're only notified if you use the SOS button.

What if I'm not tech-savvy?

SteadiDay is designed for simplicity. Large buttons, clear text, and straightforward navigation. No tech skills needed.

Why is everything free?

We want your honest feedback. Every feature is unlocked so you can try the full experience and help us improve. Your input makes a real difference.

Your data stays yours

SteadiDay stores everything on your device—not our servers. Your health information is never sold, shared, or used for advertising. We believe privacy is a right, not a feature.
