Questions?

Frequently asked questions

Is my information private?

Yes. All your data stays on your iPhone and is protected by your phone's security. We never see or store your information on our servers.

Do I need to create an account?

No account needed. Just download and start using it right away. Your data is stored safely on your device.

Is the Emergency SOS really free?

Yes, always. Emergency SOS and fall detection are free for everyone, forever. We believe safety should never have a price tag.

Can my family see my information?

Your data is private. You choose who to add as trusted contacts, and they're only notified if you use the SOS button.

What if I'm not tech-savvy?

SteadiDay is designed for simplicity. Large buttons, clear text, and straightforward navigation. No tech skills needed.

Can I try before paying?

The free version includes all safety features plus 5 medications and 3 contacts—enough to fully experience the app before upgrading.

Your data stays yours

SteadiDay stores everything on your device—not our servers. Your health information is never sold, shared, or used for advertising. We believe privacy is a right, not a feature.
